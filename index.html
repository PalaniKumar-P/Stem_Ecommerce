    Stem

    Stem is a video game digital distribution service by Valve.
    It was launched as a standalone software client in September 2003 as a
    way for Valve to provide automatic updates for their games, and expanded to include games from third-party publishers

    It also provides the user with installation and automatic updating of games, and community features such as friends lists and groups, cloud storage, and in-game voice and chat functionality.
